  General Notes:

Moved with his father to Indiana. In 1816 he went to Ohio where he married Sarah Hunt Kelsey.
"Isaac Coddington, aged 58 years, residing in said County. Swears he was drafted at Centerville, Montgomery Co, Ohio about March 15, 1813 for 6 months. Served in company of Capt. William Vanchave, Col. Hollis regiment. B.L.W. wanted." No pension (War of 1812) 1766,2305
