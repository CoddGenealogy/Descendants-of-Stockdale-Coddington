Roswell Graham CODDINGTON 2217,2596,2597

  • Born: 31 Oct 1838, Batavia, Genesee Co., New York 2217,2596
  • Died: 2 Mar 1862, Williamsport, Lycoming Co., Pennsylvania at age 23 2597
  • Buried: Batavia Cmty, Buffalo, Erie Co., New York 2598

  General Notes:

Roswell participated in the Civil War. Enlisted as Private at Batavia, NY 22 May, 1861 in Co F, 28th NY Volunteer Infantry. Taken sick at Hancock, MD, and died at Williamsport PA of typhoid fever. He never engaged the enemy. Letters to his mother survive. 2597
