Alexander CODDINGTON 2085,2268,4734

  • Born: 30 Jun 1810, Esopus, Ulster Co., New York 2268,4734
  • Marriage (1): Mahala MANNING on 27 Dec 1832 in Ithaca, Tompkins Co., New York 4733

  General Notes:

Although not mentioned in John I. Coddington's papers, it seems likely that Alexander was the son of Joseph Coddington and Meribah Harford. His birth date fits, and his putative brother Robert married Mahala's sister Margaret. Nevertheless, Alexander is absent from the records of the First Presbyterian Church in Ithaca, at least under that name.

Alexander and wife Mahala mentioned in "dower books" as being in Wisconsin territory Aug. 9, 1843 during settlement of death of John Manning, Mahala's father. 1318
