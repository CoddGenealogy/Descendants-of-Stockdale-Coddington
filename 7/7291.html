  General Notes:

Link not proven to Henry and Elizabeth, but was certainly brother to James M., and their birth years fills in gaps in the children of Henry and Elizabeth. Also, members of Henry's family also buried in Bloomsburg Cemetery.

Involved with Livingston Manor and was a Civil War Vet. Age, 27 years. Enlisted, Aug 12, 1862, at Bloomingburgh, to serve three years; mustered in as Private, Co E, 124th NY Vol Inf, Sep 5, 1862; mustered out with company, June 3, 1865, near Washington, D.C.
