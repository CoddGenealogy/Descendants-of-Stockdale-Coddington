Lyman Badgley CODDINGTON Sr 860,1581

  • Born: 8 Aug 1866, Saint Louis, St. Louis City, Missouri 860
  • Marriage (1): Adelaide Evelyn CHANDLER on 20 Nov 1902 in Roselle, Union Co., New Jersey 1581
  • Died: 10 Dec 1953, New Providence, Union Co., New Jersey at age 87

  General Notes:

Lyman started one of the nation's largest and most famous Rose businesses, and invented several varieties of Roses. His sons, Fred and Lyman Jr both ran the business when they grew up. Lyman's Rose business was in New Providence, NJ. Presbyterian. Republican. Trustee and clerk of the Sunday school (1911). 860
