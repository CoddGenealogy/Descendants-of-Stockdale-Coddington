Abraham CODDINGTON 338,1402

  • Born: 31 Dec 1799, New York, New York Co., New York 338
  • Marriage (1): Martha Bruce FORBES on 31 Aug 1828
  • Died: 27 Feb 1857, Yonkers, Westchester Co., New York at age 57 660,1376
  • Buried: Saint John's Cmty, Yonkers, Westchester Co., New York 1376

  General Notes:

Born at Provost, Now Franklin St, NYC. He learned the carpenter's trade, and afterwards became a grocer in Greenwich St, NYC, but returned to his trade about the time of his marriage. He and his wife were members of the M. E. church. In 1848, having acquired a competency, he moved to Yonkers, where he died. (From Rev. Herbert G. Coddington, D.D.) 338
