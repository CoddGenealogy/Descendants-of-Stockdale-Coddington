  General Notes:

Born on Chambers St.
He was a class leader and Trustee of the Spring Street Methodist Episcopal Church of Sing Sing, NY 1 Aug 1837 and held these positions for nearly 50 yrs. He was also treasurer of that church for over 40 years. He was also Trustee of the village for some time. In his early life he was a hatter by trade and later kept a market in Sing Sing. In later life lived on his income. 1167,1170
