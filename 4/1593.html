Wesley Isaiah CODDINGTON 719

  • Born: 19 Jan 1895, Mount Horeb, Somerset Co., New Jersey 719
  • Marriage (1): Anna Elizabeth SAUERWEIN on 15 Jan 1921 in Plainfield, Union Co., New Jersey 1585
  • Died: 12 Nov 1927, Mount Horeb, Somerset Co., New Jersey at age 32
  • Buried: Mount Horeb Cmty, Mount Horeb, Somerset Co., New Jersey

  General Notes:

Farmer, Methodist, Republican. Two versions of how Wesley died: 1) according to Donald Brewer (his half nephew) he was kicked in the head by a mule/horse; 2) according to Wesley, Jr (his son), he fell off a wagon and was given bad medicine. 719


Wesley married Anna Elizabeth SAUERWEIN, daughter of Joseph SAUERWEIN and Anna, on 15 Jan 1921 in Plainfield, Union Co., New Jersey.1585 (Anna Elizabeth SAUERWEIN was born about 1890 in Elizabeth, Union Co., New Jersey and died in 1965.)
