  General Notes:

Seems to have taken his family to Michigan ca. 1840, and returned.
Something wrong in the dates listed in Tompkins Co. New York Church Records (q.v.) as "Marguerite" is listed as being dismissed from the church in 1826 as "widow of Robert" but he did not die until 1845. 2088
