Charles Wesley CODDINGTON 186,719,1587

  • Born: 2 Nov 1897, Newark, Essex Co., New Jersey 186,719,1587
  • Marriage (1): Elsie Caroline COLLINSON on 6 Dec 1922 in New Monmouth, Monmouth Co., New Jersey 1587
  • Died: 24 May 1964, Selbyville, Sussex Co., Delaware at age 66

   Cause of his death was Heart Attack.

  General Notes:

He was a successful farmer in the New Monmouth, NJ, area between 1923 and 1936. In that year the family moved to Oneonta, NY, where he was invited to join the staff of a branch of the corporation organized by his brother, Clarence, the Beacon Milling Co. In 1947 they moved to Scotia, NY, in the same line of work. Two years later he was asked to manage a Beacon store in Selbyville, DE. Later, he was requested to take the position of traffic manager at still another branch of the Beacon Milling Co, this in Laurel, DE, about 25 miles from Selbyville and his home.


Charles married Elsie Caroline COLLINSON on 6 Dec 1922 in New Monmouth, Monmouth Co., New Jersey.1587 (Elsie Caroline COLLINSON was born on 4 Oct 1898 and died on 16 Oct 1990 in Oneonta, Otsego Co., New York.)
