  General Notes:

I, Mrs. Vera Coddington Smith, resident of Manoa, Manoa Road, Delaware Co., Pa., married Mr. Whitney Smith, January 30, 1911; hereby apply for membership in The Society of the Sons and Daughters of the Pilgrims by right of descent from Governor William Coddington, came to America, 1630; born 1601, at Lincolnshire, England; died 1678, at Rhode Island. Service: Founder of Massachusetts Bay Colony; Assistant, 1629-1637; Treasurer, 1634-35; Deputy, 1636, 1637; founder of Portsmouth, R. I., 1638; Newport, 1639; Judge, 1638-40; Governor, 1640-1647; Assistant, 1647, 1660,1667; President of Colony, 1640; Governor, 1674, 1676, 1678.
1. I am the daughter of David Coddington, born March 24, 1860; living 1927, and his wife Sarah Brown, born April 28, 1864; married 1882.
2. The said David Coddington was the son of Garret Vanderveer Coddington, born 1833, died 1899, and his wife Jane Burnet; married June 18, 1856.
3. The said Garret Vanderveer Coddington was the son of David Coddington, born July 31, 1801; died August 5, 1874, and his wife Deborah Vanderveer, born February 14, 1807; died February 18, 1882; married April 27, 1826.
4. The said David Coddington was the son of Moses Coddington, born May 21, 1769; died April 14, 1843; and his wife Elizabeth Bonney, died April 11, 1849; married 1794.
5. The said Moses Coddington was the son of David Coddington, born 1730, died 1788, and his wife Ann Stone, died 1807; married 1750.
6. The said David Coddington was the son of John Coddington, born 1706, died 1758, and his wife Margaret Edgar, died after 1758.
7. The said John Coddington was the son of John Coddington, born November 8, 1677; died 1738/9, and his wife Elizabeth Dennis, born 1678, died after 1738/9; married 1705.
8. The said John Coddington was the son of John Coddington, born 1656, died after 1700, and his wife Hannah Gardner, died- September 20, 1690; married before 1677.
9. The said John Coddington was the son of Governor William Coddington, born 1601, died 1678, and his wife Ann Brinley, born 1628, died May 9, 1708; married January, 1649.
