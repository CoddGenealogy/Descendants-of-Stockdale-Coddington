  General Notes:

Born at 31 Jones St.
Living (1904) in Yonkers, NY. Insurance agent; Protestant; Republican; never married. (From Rev. Herbert G. Coddington, D. D.).
Note. In the 1880 census Abram is listed twice, once aged 29 under his father, occupation bookkeeper (p. 544C). Another Abram appears on p. 492C, aged 29, occupation bookkeeper, with an extensive family. It seems likely that H. G. Coddington was incorrect when he wrote that Abram never married. 1352,1379
