  • Died: 17 Sep 1871, Jamestown, Boone Co., Indiana about age 68 3758,3759

  General Notes:

Israel, Charles, and Thomas Paterson all listed in 1810 Census for Montgomery Co. KY w sons under 10. A Charles Patterson left a will (2 Dec 1814, 7 Feb 1816) in Franklin Co. Kentucky (Will Book 3, p. 222) that mentions many people, none of whom are a Jacob, Nancy or any names of their children.
His middle name may have been Seaborn.
In the 1830 Census, he owned one male slave under 10.
Relocated prior to 1860 to Lebanon, Boone Co. Indiana. He and Nancy witnessed a will there. 3758,3760
