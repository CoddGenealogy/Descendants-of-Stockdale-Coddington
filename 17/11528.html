  General Notes:

Widow of (1) Ambrose Callis and (2) John Wilcox. Aunt of Ann Sorrell Earle, the first wife of Charles Buck I. Her son Thomas wrote of her "She was a strict moralist, and instilled into my mind a great reverence for the Supreme Being".

-- charts of H. N. Buck, 1965

She had three sons and five daughters by her previous marriages.

-- W. P. Buck, 1986.

Thomas Buck wrote of his mother, "She was born in Westmoreland County, VA leaving all her connections, but one sister (wife of Samuel Earle) to follow my father to this country. Her maiden name was Letitia Sorrell"


Letitia married Charles BUCK, son of Thomas BUCK and Unknown, in 1749.5021 (Charles BUCK was born about 1710 in , , Virginia and died in Mar 1771 in , Warren Co., Virginia.)
