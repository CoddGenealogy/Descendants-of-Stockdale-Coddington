  General Notes:

Thomas Poindexter Heir 19/10/1721 Breechin, James, 19 Oct. 1721; 6 April
1722. My late wife Ann; sons William and James land at falls of Potomac; to
Mr. John Rele 50 acres; to Dennis Lynsey 100 acres of land; to Thomas
Poindexter 300 acres of land; dau. Anna and Jane land; to James and Anna
Sorrell 1 hogshead tobacco each; kinsman Thomas Sorrell a mourning ring;
wife and Capt. George Turberville exrs., the latter to have a mourning ring;
wife Sarah personal estate.

This will as probated 1716. Read John Erwin will. John Sorrell wife died before 1716 by what the will states.

Westmoreland Wills-abstracts: ERWIN, John, 10 April 1716; 30 May 1716. My godchildren John son of George and Anne South; Frances dau. of John Sorrell and Anne his late wife; Jemimah dau. of John & Hannah Awbrey; John son of Charles and Temperance Lucas and Elizabeth dau. of Thomas & Elizabeth Sorrell, all to have 2 year's schooling each; to Wm. Clark my overseer; friend John Awbrey land purchased of John Hobson late dec'd; friend Thomas Sorrell plantation & to be exrs.) 5778
