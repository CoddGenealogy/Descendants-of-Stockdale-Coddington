  General Notes:

"He returned from Illinois with his mother after the death of his father. He secured work on a farm as a laborer and manager. Following this occupation until late in life when he married Ellen Morgan, a spinster of Okeana, Ohio. After this marriage he purchased a small farm west of Liberty, Indiana, and remained there until his wife's death some ten years later. He disposed of this farm and became and inmate of the Odd Fellows Home at Greensburg, Indiana. Died and was buried there...He had belong to that order for 40 years." 1017
