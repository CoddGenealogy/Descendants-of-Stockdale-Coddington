  General Notes:

Yeoman, boatman. Tombstone says 67 years, actually 63.
On 7 Jan 1716/17 obligated himself to give his daughter Agnes a Negro wench [slave]named Villet when she (Agnes) reached the age of 18, or, if Villet should die before then, then he would pay Agnes £20.


A member of the founding settlement of Woodbridge, New Jersey (Clayton, 1882). 347,497
