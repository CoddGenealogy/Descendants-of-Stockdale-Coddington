Franklin Pierce CODDINGTON 770

  • Born: 5 Oct 1864, Warren Twp., Somerset Co., New Jersey 899
  • Marriage (1): Ida May CODDINGTON in Mount Horeb, Somerset Co., New Jersey 860
  • Died: 6 Jan 1943, Mount Horeb, Somerset Co., New Jersey at age 78
  • Buried: Mount Horeb Cmty, Mount Horeb, Somerset Co., New Jersey

  General Notes:

Franklin changed their family name to Codington - with one D. He had told Lora that this was the 'old fashioned' way the name had been spelled. Franklin was a farmer, and his son & daughter (Lester and Lora) continued to live on their farm until he died (Lora is still alive in June 2000 and still lives on that farm). Lester went to college and became a teacher, still farmed on the side, and later switched from dairy to fruits (mostly peaches). Franklin had bought farms or houses in other areas (NY state for example) and rented out these other properties.

  Noted events in his life were:

• Census, 1880, Warren Twp., Somerset Co., New Jersey. 778


Franklin married Ida May CODDINGTON, daughter of John Wesley CODDINGTON and Mary Jane COON, in Mount Horeb, Somerset Co., New Jersey.860 (Ida May CODDINGTON was born on 22 Dec 1871 in Mount Horeb, Somerset Co., New Jersey,860 died on 9 Feb 1934 in Mount Horeb, Somerset Co., New Jersey and was buried in Mount Horeb Cmty, Mount Horeb, Somerset Co., New Jersey.)
