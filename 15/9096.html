  General Notes:

"Captain John Duvall, m abt 1686 to Elizabeth Jones. They had 11 children. One of the more important military officers of the Provence. In 1704, he and his brother, Mareen the elder, were granted "Duvalls Delight", a patent of 3108 acres, located at the mouth of Rogues Harbor. It was one of the true manors in Anne Arundel Co. and is listed in Alice Norris Parran's "Register of Maryland Heraldic Families".
Maryland State Archives (ARMD 40-88; AALR IH#1-66): John of AA Co. m. by 19 Aug. 1685/17 Aug 1689, Eliza, dau of William Jones" 2888


John married Mary Elizabeth JONES, daughter of William JONES and Elizabeth, on 19 Aug 1685 in , Anne Arundel Co., Maryland.342 (Mary Elizabeth JONES was born in 1660 in Abington, Anne Arundel Co., Maryland 2888 and died after 1716 in Queen Annes, Anne Arundel Co., Maryland 2888.)
