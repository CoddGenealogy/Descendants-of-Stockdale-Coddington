  General Notes:

Christian Miller, (also called Christopher-Chrisley in some records).Though legal proof of parentage is still lacking, there is reasonable evidence to support the theory that he was son of Henry and Catherine (Ely) Miller. Both buried Richland Cemetery, Union Co., IN. Christian born ca. 1773 (from will); married to Catherine _______ ca. 1792, probably E. TN. Her parentage unknown. She was born ca. 1769;died 11 Oct 1846, buried Upper Mt. Zion Cemetery, Boon County,Indiana.
