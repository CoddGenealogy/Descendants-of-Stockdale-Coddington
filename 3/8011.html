Moses Love CODDINGTON 38,4332

  • Born: 25 Sep 1807, New York, New York Co., New York 38
  • Marriage (1): Eliza Catherine DUPUY on 20 Jul 1833 in Liberty, Sullivan Co., New York 38,4332
  • Died: 8 Jun 1884 at age 76
  • Buried: Brunswick Cmty, Shawangunk, Ulster Co., New York 3170,4333

  General Notes:

In 1850 census, living next to Evens family.
Enlisted at New York, NY 14 Nov 1862 Private, Co. A, 156th NY Inf. Discharged 14 Sep 1864 Carver Hospital, Washington DC. Civil War Pension No. WC 326,860. Moses and three sons, Edward, George, and Levi, fought in the Civil War. Levi died at the battle of Saratoga.

In her letter to Freeman Worth Gardner, Bernice Lewis Swainson says Moses served in the revolution and she incorrectly reports his death as 1844 instead of 1884, but correctly reports Eliza Catherine's death date of 6 Dec. 1893. 4334,4335,4336
