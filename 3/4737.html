  General Notes:

The 1810 census for Ulysses, Seneca Co. has a Jacob, 16-25, with a female 16-25. Presumably this is Jacob and Sally Arnold, although supposedly they did not marry until 1811. If so, Jacob probably travelled with his uncles Joseph and David to join their cousin John 1778. Died on his farm, Section 22, Armada Twp., Macomb Co, Michigan. 1116
