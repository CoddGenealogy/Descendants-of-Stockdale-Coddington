  General Notes:

David served in the New York State Militia during the Revolutionary War. The 1790 census gives a Benjamin that fits this family partially, except for 3 females, two of whom may be Mary and Elizabeth, but the third is unexplained. Living in Goshen, New York in 1789. Possibly died in Huntington, NY, aged 85, 25 July, 1848 (ex obit Long Islander 28 July, 1848). 2537,2538
