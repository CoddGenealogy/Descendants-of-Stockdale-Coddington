  General Notes:

Lady Castleton and Baroness of Castleton. Catholic. Married a protestant, so Church gave her title and lands to a cousin. Correspondence about this by Lady Walpole. Information told to Eleanor (Capen) Coddington by Lily Chadbourne (ex Papers of Elizabeth Lee (Ayres) Coddington.
Buried in Woodlawn Cemetery, NYC, Lot 5669, Plot: Lake View, Sec. 75, Area 300', Deed No. 5932, Aff. No. 12,333.

[Note: James Saunderson lord viscount Castleton of the kingdom of Ireland was created by king George the first 1714 lord Saunderson, 1716 viscount Castleton, and afterwards earl of Castleton, which titles became extinct at his death 23 May 1723.] 3782
