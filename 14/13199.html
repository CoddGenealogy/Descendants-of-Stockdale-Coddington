  General Notes:

No unequivocal proof identifies George Washington Coddington as the George Washington Coddington in the 1870 census record in New Jersey. However, this George Washington did name children Robert Ellsworth and Harry, and the New Jersey George Washington had sibs named Elmer Ellsworth and Harry. Also Wilma Coddington Foster testifies that her grandfather emigrated from New Jersey in the late 1900's.

"My grandfather, George Washington Coddington, emigrated from New Jersey to Hamilton, Ont. in the late 19th c. He was looking for work in steel mill [sic], I believe. My grandmother had emigrated from Ireland and taught school in Ont. until they married and moved to Montreal where, as far as I know, all the children were born. Five boys reached maturity and one girl." All are now deceased except my aunt who lives in Montreal- Lena Homes. ...Harry, Bob and Norma all moved to the Winnepeg area in the 1920's and 30's. Their children were raised in Winnipeg. Lorne Coddington moved to Vancouver in the 50', where he still resides, Kathleen lives in Quebec. Ruth lives in Winnipeg. The others live in Ontario and Quebec." Wilma (Coddington) Forster 5460,5461
