  General Notes:

Las Vegas Review-Journal (NV) - Saturday, March 5, 2005
Deceased Name: LORNA CODDINGTON
Lorna Pearl Coddington, 91, a homemaker, of La Habra Heights, Calif., passed away Wednesday, March 2, 2005. She was born Jan. 2, 1914, in Preston, Idaho, and resided in Las Vegas for 25 years. Lorna was a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. She was also a member of the relief society room for the Civic Center Ward. Lorna is survived by her daughter and son-in-law, Klis Coddington Ruesch and Lamar Ruesch of Las Vegas; son and daughter-in-law, Boyd and Jo Coddington of La Habra Heights, Calif.; sister, Delma Checketts of Preston, Idaho; and 11 grandchildren, 12 great-grandchildren and one great great-grandchild. Visitation will be from 10 a.m.-noon Monday, March 7, with services immediately following, both at Palm Mortuary, 1325 N. Main St. Interment to follow at Palm Memorial Park-Las Vegas. Contributions may be made to the Elwyn Foundation. 5967
