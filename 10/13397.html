Freeman Lewis CORRINGTON 114,1937

  • Born: 1817, Deerfield Twp. Cmty, Mason, Warren Co., Ohio 1937
  • Marriage (1): Elizabeth BELT on 7 Jul 1843 114,1937
  • Died: 14 Feb 1860, Geneva, Adams Co., Indiana at age 43 114

  General Notes:

"My grandfather left Mason in the forties for nothern [sic] Ind. and died in 1860 when my father was six years old, my fathers knowledge of his family was very limited aside from his father born at Mason Ohio."

Freeman changed his name from Corrington to Carrington during the Civil War
