  General Notes:

The Rev. Robert Elkin and his wife Sarah, of the Providence Church, Winchester, Clark Co. Kentucky, had a daughter Sarah who married a Reuben H. Elkin in Rockcastle Co., KY on 12 Dec 1802.
Their son Ezekiel (b. ca 1785 in Clark Co., Kentucky) married Nancy Acton and also had a son named Reuben H. Elkin.
Either Reuben H. Elkin may have been the father of Reuben H. (1815-1896).
The 1820 Mount Vernon, Rockcastle Co. census lists Reuben H. Elkin with 2 10-16 males, 2 16-18 males, 1 18-26 male (self), and one 26-45 female.
The 1830 Mt. Vernon, Rockcastle Co. census lists Reuben H. Elkin with (image not found on ancestry.com).
The 1840 Pulaski, Pulaski Co. census lists Reuben H. Elkin with 1 15-20 male, 1 60-70 male, 1 15-20 female, and 1 50-60 female.
