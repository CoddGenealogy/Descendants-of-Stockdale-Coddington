  General Notes:

Emma married John Jepson on May 7, 1656.
BOSTON BIRTHS, BAPTISMS, MARRIAGES, AND DEATHS, 1630-1699
1656, page 56. John Jephson & Emm Coddington widow were married 7th--3rd mo. by Major Humph. Atharton.
"After the death of her second husband in 1688, Emm became a midwife, and she testified before the Supreme Judicial Court of Massachusetts in a good many cases involving charges of fornication against various couples who had produced untimely offspring." [Supreme Judicial Court of Massachusetts, dockets 4312, 4343, 4432, 98547, 98548, dates 8 Apr. 1696- 6 Jan 169/8" ex Coddington, J. I. Additions and Corrections...p. 95.
