  General Notes:

Notes from "Isaac Allerton of the Mayflower": On 7 Mar 1711/12 Lidia was appointed adm. of the est. of William Harlow of Plymouth. A settlement dated 21 Jun 1712, names widow Lidia Harlow; dau. Elizabeth Doten is deceased leaving one son Thomas Doten; eldest son Thomas Harlow; second son William Harlow; third son Robert Harlow; fourth son Isaac; second dau. Mary; third dau. Lidia; youngest dau. Rebecca (no surnames).



On 15 April 1683 William Harlow and Lydia his wife were called before the church because their child was born within six weeks after marriage.
