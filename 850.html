  General Notes:

Coal and Lumber Merchant of Rahway and later Woodbridge. Lived in a fine house on Irving Street (in Rahway) just off Milton Ave (per John I. Coddington).
The 1880 Census for Buffalo, Erie Co., New York lists a Henry Inman, 46 b. NY, wife Mary 41 b. Canada, daughter Mary T. 17, b. NY, daughter Charlotte 14, b. NY, daughter May 9, b. NY, AND Caroline Coddington 17, b. Michigan, and William Coddington 9, nephew b. Michigan. Who are these? Henry Sr. did have a son Henry Jr., but the dates are off, and he was supposedly living in Kansas during these years, and Smith did not have children of these ages or names. Did Coddingtons intermarry with Inman's twice? 1064,1066
