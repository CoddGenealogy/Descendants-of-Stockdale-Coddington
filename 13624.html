I. Major Charles J. Higinbotham, Medical Reserve of U. S. A.; married July 31, 1910 to Emily Jessup Wilkinson (1879-1921); hereby apply for membership in The Society of the Sons and Daughters of the Pilgrims by right of descent from John Sanford, born 1600, died 1655. Service: President of Portsmouth and Newport, R. I., 1653; founder of Charter Colony of Portsmouth-
1. I am the son of Horace G. Higinbotham, born 1855, died 1922, and his wife Dr. Lillian Green Starkweather, born 1859, living 1928; married December 25, 1877.
2. The said Dr. Lillian S. G. Higinbotham was the daughter of John Leonard Starkweather, born 1830, died 1910, and his wife Sarah Palmer Green, born 1831, died 1892; married November 28, 1855.
3. The said Sarah Palmer Green-Starkweather was the daughter of Deacon Paul Green, born 1803, died 1883, and his wife Sarah Palmer, born 1805, died 1891.
4. The said Sarah Palmer was the daughter of Luther Palmer, born 1774, died 1815, and his wife Sarah Kenyan, born 1776, died 1815.
5. The said Luther Palmer (1774-1815) was the son of Col. Elias Sanford Palmer, born 1742, died 1821, and his wife Phebe Palmer (a cousin), born 1742, died 1793; married 1761.
6. The said Col. Elias S. Palmer (1742-1821) was the son of Lieut. Ichabod Palmer, Jr., born 1703, died 1744, and his wife Elizabeth Noyes, born 1706, died 1780; married 1723.
7. The said Elizabeth Noyes (1706-1780) was the daughter of Capt. Thomas Noyes, born 1679, died 1755, and his wife Elizabeth Sanford, born 1683; married 1705.
8. The said Elizabeth Sanford, born 1683, was the daughter of Gov. Peleg Sanford of R. I., born 1639, died 1701, and his wife Mary Coddington, born 1654, died 1692 or 1693; married 1674.
9. The said Gov. Peleg Sanford (1639-1701) was the son of President John Sanford, born 1600, died 1655, and his wife Bridget Hutchinson, born 1618, died 1696; married 1636
