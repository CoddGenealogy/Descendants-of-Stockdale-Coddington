  General Notes:

Always used July 4, 1870 as her birth date, but 1870 and 1880 census record the year as 1868.

She received a cable from Julian Wright in Paris on Sept. 18, 1922 saying "Condition unimproved this morning after bad night temperature one hundred and five six tenths you must come told her you sailing Tuesday." Alice applied for a passort the same day. Violet died Oct. 12 of a septic throat.

Reentered the United States on 24 Oct., 1922 at Ellis Island, New York, on ship Majestic, departed from Cherbourg, Mance, France, with grandsons William Draper and Dave Hennen Jr.
Met her second husband, William Draper at Bar Harbor Maine, where the Cushman's had a summer house.
