   Another name for Wilhelm was William MUELLER.

  General Notes:

Family history is that William belonged to house of von Hammerstein, born 1792, got in trouble politically, left Germany as a late teenager. Not found in 1860 census, so probably emigrated after 1860. 1870 census states born in "Prussia." 1900 census states he arrived in the U.S. in 1859, and rented the house he lived in. 1930 census record for Alice Muller gives place of birth of father as "North Germany."
Calvary Cemetery, lot 984, Sect. 20, St. Louis, MO "in the name of William Mueller"
