  General Notes:

Apparently James, Stephen, Ophelia, and Eliza Ann Coddington accompanied brothers Martin Luther, Reuben Purdy, and Chester Towner to Michigan in 1830's. The latter stayed on in Michigan, but the former returned and joined their old Church in 1839. The latter were dismissed from the church in 1840. 3928
