  General Notes:

"He was a farmer and brick-maker." [HGC, 1907) His will is at Surrogate's Office, New Brunswick, NJ. David & Millard, Executors. Date of will: 23 July, 1805. Parts of will:--Best feather bed to wife, Mary; also warming pan, a painted white pine chest and other household furniture. Other movable property to be sold at auction, and divided between three daughters. To David and Millard, one half each of dwelling house and seven acres. Directs his sons to pay $25. apiece to their mother each year. Witnesses: Robert Moores, William Harriot, Thomas Moores. 162,178
