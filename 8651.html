  • Died: 12 Sep 1879, Rochester (Accord), Ulster Co., New York at age 72 2522

  General Notes:

Farmer and stonecutter. Ancestor of the Coddingtons of Waterbury, Connecticut

Information re the wife and children of William furnished by Marian Whitford, July 2000 (presently unverified).
"William Christopher Coddington lived at Ball's Eddy on the Delware River (may have been the Susquehanna River). He and his family were driven out by the Indians. He and his wife and all of their children except two died during the journey to Ulster Co., NY. (Note by JIC: this may refer to Christopher Coddington and his wife Maria Oosterhoudt, and their departure from Northumberland Co.). Among the children of this family were (1) Joseph, (2) Christopher, and (3) Daniel. The above copied from Herbert G. Coddington's papers." 66
