  General Notes:

In 1870 census, aged 12, living in Phineas T. Barnum's house in New York City, where his mother worked as a housekeeper.
Pastor of Grove Hall Church, Boston, moved to Winter Hill Universalist Church, Somerville, MA in 1888. His daughter, Gladys Coddington was president of the Busy Bees, a church committee. 2809
