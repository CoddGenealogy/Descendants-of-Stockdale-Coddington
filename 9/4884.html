  General Notes:

A shoemaker. Daniel Schram Coddington and all of his five sons, George H., William Albert, Lewis., James D. and David H. fought in the Civil War.
Enlisted 17 June 1861 at Wyreka, Putnam Co. Missouri, age 30 in Co. B, 18th Missouri Infantry, and discharged 3 July 1862 for disability (extreme attacks of diarrhea).
In Hospital in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania 1 May 1862.
Enlisted (again) 21 May 1864 at Corydon Iowa in Co. H. 46th Iowa Infantry, and mustered out 23 Sep 1864 at Davenport Iowa.
Died at home of son, Lewis C. Coddington. 1746,2928
