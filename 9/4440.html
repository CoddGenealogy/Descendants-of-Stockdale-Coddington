  General Notes:

Calvin and Sarah moved later to the town of Howard, Steuben County, [New York] where Calvin was apparently engaged in clearing timber land. He was accidentally killed by a falling tree late in December, 1836, and his body was not found by searching parties for three weeks, or some time in January, 1837. 2692
