  • Died: 10 Feb 1882, Westfield, Bureau Co., Illinois at age 56 2754
  • Buried: Oakland Cmty, Princeton, Bureau Co., Illinois 2691

  General Notes:

"He enlisted into the Civil War on the 3rd of August, 1862 as a private in the 93rd Illinois Infantry and was discharge at Rock Island, Illinois on the 10th of February, 1865. He was wounded at Chattanooga on the 25th of November, 1863 and his left hand was rendered useless. After his discharge he became 1st Sergeant of the 4th Veterans Reserve Corp., Illinois Militia. He received a pension on account of his Civil War service, and after his death his wife Amanda applied for a widows pension, and received it. He died at the age of 55.(The above is derived from Civil War Pension Records W.C.292,034)" 851,2691
