  General Notes:

Cornelia Crow was best friend of Hattie Hosmer, later the protege and companion of Charlotte Cushman. Hattie met Cornelia while studying anatomy at St. Louis University in order to pursue a career as a sculptress. Hattie saw Charlotte's portrayal of Hamlet in Boston in 1850 and it changed her life.
Hattie joined Charlotte Cushman and Matilda M. Hays (who was the British translator of George Sand), and together they formed what they called the "Jolly Bachelors." Charlotte and Matilda dressed like men from the waist up and Hattie dressed like a man from the waist up and down.
They met and socialized with the Browning's (Elizabeth Barrett and Robert), whose son was later to marry Fanny Coddington. 2594
