  • Born: 18 Dec 1838, Bernards Twp., Somerset Co., New Jersey 2513,5099
  • Marriage (1): John Aaron CODDINGTON on 2 Apr 1859 in Piscataway, Middlesex Co., New Jersey
  • Died: 27 Mar 1927, Kilbourne, Mason Co., Illinois at age 88
  • Buried: 29 Mar 1927, Bethel Cmty, Saidora, Mason Co., Illinois

  General Notes:

After Anna's divorce from John in Mason County, Illinois on August 1893 she lived in a duplex in Manito, Tazewell County, Illinois, until about 1922. From then she lived with her daughter until her death.

  Noted events in her life were:

• Census, 1860, Warren Twp., Somerset Co., New Jersey. 789

• Engagement, 1880, Sand Prairie, Tazewell Co., Illinois. 2513

• Census, 16 Jun 1870, New Providence, Union Co., New Jersey. 5099


Anna married John Aaron CODDINGTON, son of Aaron CODDINGTON and Sarah Ann CORYELL, on 2 Apr 1859 in Piscataway, Middlesex Co., New Jersey.
