Samuel MOORE 347

  • Born: Cir 1630, Newburyport, Essex Co., Massachusetts
  • Marriage (1): Hannah Plumer on 3 May 1653 in Newburyport, Essex Co., Massachusetts 347,4524
  • Marriage (2): Mary ILSLEY on 12 Sep 1656 in Newburyport, Essex Co., Massachusetts 347,4524

  • Died: 27 May 1688, Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey about age 58 347

  General Notes:

Samuel Moore was the first town clerk of Woodbridge, New Jersey and occupied the office from 1668-1688.
Moved to Woodbridge from Newbury, Massachusetts with "five daughters, three sons, and two slaves, each 60 acres." (Myers, 1995:650) 347,4934

  Noted events in his life were:

• Fact: Assistant to the Township Court, 1671, Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey. 347

• Fact: Deputy to the General Assembly, 1668, Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey. 347

• Fact: Deputy to the General Assembly, 1671, Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey. 347

• Fact: President of the Township Court, 1672, Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey. 347

• Fact: Assistant to the Township Court, 1675, Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey. 347

• Election: overseer of the poor, 1683, Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey. 347

• Fact: Assistant to the Township Court, 1683, Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey. 347

• Fact: Appointed to petition the new proprietors to confirm the town charter, 18 Apr 1686, Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey. 347

• Fact: Appointed to meet with a committee from Elizabethtown to determine the best route for a road between the two towns, 1 Oct 1686, Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey. 347

• Will, 13 Nov 1684, Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey. 4935


Samuel married Hannah Plumer on 3 May 1653 in Newburyport, Essex Co., Massachusetts 347.,4524 (Hannah Plumer died in Dec 1654 347.). The cause of her death was probably childbirth.


Samuel next married Mary ILSLEY, daughter of William ILSLEY and Barbara STEVENS, on 12 Sep 1656 in Newburyport, Essex Co., Massachusetts 347.,4524 (Mary ILSLEY was born circa 1638 in Newburyport, Essex Co., Massachusetts 4524 and died on 27 May 1688 in Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey 4524.)


Samuel next married Ann Jacques in Dec 1678.347
