  General Notes:

"CODDINGTON Capt. Edward, at Newport, in 76th year; had been for many years an officer in the customs department, was a descendant of Gov. William, one of the early settlers of the town, March 1, 1816"
The assignment of Nathaniel, Isaac, and John to Capt. Edward is speculative. The birth dates of these putative sons are reasonably well established. Rhode Island records document two Coddington marriages in the mid 1700's, Capt. Edward and his brother Nathaniel who married Mary Oxx.. Of these two women, only Ann Nixon would have been young enough to have children in the 1770's.
Finally, the 1800 and 1810 censuses list Edward as head of household with approximately the right family members. 5548
