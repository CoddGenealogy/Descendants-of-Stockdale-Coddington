  General Notes:

Contractor and builder. Appointed auctioneer for Ontario Co., New York, 1831. According to HGC, "147. had 2 sisters and three brothers. 1) Maria who lived in Buffalo and is dead. 2. another sister who lived (+and perhaps now lives) in Ithaca. 3. Benjamin lives in Kentucky. 4. John lives in New York City. 5. Richard lives in New York City. Samuel O. Coddington was the brother of the 1st George's father." 632,4602,4764
