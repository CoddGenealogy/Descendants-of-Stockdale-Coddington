  General Notes:

Born in Allegany Co., Maryland, E. R. Coddington moved to Perry Co., Ohio in 1816, where he married E. J. Kennedy in 1834. Moved into Indiana in 1854 when country was new; lived in a log cabin and cleared the land. A farmer on 135 acres, Republican, formerly a Whig. (per Rev. Herbert G. Coddington and Burley Benton Coddington). 472,1632
