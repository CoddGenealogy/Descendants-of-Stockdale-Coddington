  General Notes:

"In his will, dated 13 May 1803, which is on record in the Surrogate's Office, New York City, he is described as a 'taylor' of New York City, living at 176 Greenwich St. In this instrument his father Uzziah, his brother Moses, and his wife Mary, are mentioned, and several small children are referred to whose names are not given. He leaves to his wife the house at 176 Greenwich St, and the rent of the house at the corner of Dey and Greenwich Streets." 328,591
