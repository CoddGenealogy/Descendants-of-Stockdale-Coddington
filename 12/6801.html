  General Notes:

Soon after 1920 Margaret and her youngest daughter Caroline moved to Akron, Ohio to live with her eldest daughter Margaret Ayres Douthitt. After Margaret married Fred Amon, Margaret (senior) and Caroline moved to Staunton, Virginia, where they taught in the school for the deaf and blind. Later Caroline taught in the school for the deaf in Knoxville, Tennessee. Mrs. Douthitt went to Gooding, Idaho where she taught in the school for the blind until 1932 when she retired. Thereafter she lived with her daughters, generally with Caroline in Mississippi during the winter and Margaret in Massachusetts during the summer. 3486
