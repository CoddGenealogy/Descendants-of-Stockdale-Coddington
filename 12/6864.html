  General Notes:

Baptized by Rev. L. D. Parker.
Was an artist who carved many pieces of furniture which she gave to various members of the family. Had one stillborn child and adopted two sons. Taught art at De Pauw University. Asked her sister-in-law Louise Stamm Ayres to give her firstborn child, Elizabeth, because she couldn't have children and said, "You can always have more." 282,3482,3540
