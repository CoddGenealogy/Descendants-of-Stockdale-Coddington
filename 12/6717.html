  General Notes:

Daily Pantagraph, Bloomington, ILL., Thursday, Jyly 16, 1931 WALKER OFFICIATES at DELROY WEDDING Mayor Jimmie Walker of New York city officiated at the marriage Wednesday of Irene Delroy, stage and screen star, to William Liseter Austin Jr. of Atlantic Beach Long Island, according to details of the service received by The Pantagraph late Wednesday. Miss Delroy, as Josephine Lucille Sanders, spent her girlhood in Bloomington. The marriage was performed in the Hotel Pierre, New York, and the attendants were Miss Anne Austin, sister of the bridegroom and Charles N. Talbot Jr., mayor of Island Park, Long Island. Mr. Austin is president of the Island Park and Atlantic Beach Development companies. After a European wedding trip, Mr. and Mrs. Austin will live at the Hotel Pierre and Atlantic Park. Miss Delroy's parents, Mr. and Mrs. Royal W. Sanders, Peoria, were in New York for the ceremony. 3395
