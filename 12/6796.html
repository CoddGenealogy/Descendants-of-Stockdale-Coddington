  General Notes:

The link to Benjamin Jameson is not proven but Eliza Jameson did marry Hiram's brother, Lewis. Hiram named his first child "Benjamin Jameson," who was the father of Eliza, and her putative sister, Frances Prue.
Jeremiah Ayres wrote in his diary, "Mrs. D. died 6 o'clock in evening, a victim to the absurdities of the allopathic treatment." 3516,3519
