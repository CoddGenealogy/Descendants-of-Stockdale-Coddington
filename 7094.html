  General Notes:

A Quaker from 1708-1744/45, failed to carry rails and posts as requested, and also married a non-quaker and was "intemperate." Was disowned by Quakers on 1/21/1744/45.

In 1715 served in the NJ Militia Regt under Col. Thomas Farmer together with his brothers. 197


James married Abigail CONGER, daughter of John BELCONGER and Sarah CAWOOD, on 18 Dec 1706 in Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey.197 (Abigail CONGER was born on 8 May 1690 in Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey 197,2033 and died before 1733 197.)


James next married Elizabeth on 1 Sep 1733 in Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey.3567 (Elizabeth died Nov 1744-Dec 1744 in Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey.)


James next married Elizabeth DENNIS, daughter of Jonathan DENNIS and Rachel MOORE, about 5 Feb 1745 in Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey.197 (Elizabeth DENNIS was born on 8 Jan 1678 in Cohansey, Alloway Twp., Salem Co., New Jersey,203 died after 9 Jan 1739 in Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey 198 and was buried in Woodbridge, Middlesex Co., New Jersey.)
